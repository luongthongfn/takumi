About

Takumi YAMAMOTO

Founder of « Takumi YAMAMOTO »
Born in Tokyo

Takumi has gained experience as a car designer in France, UK and Japan.
In France, he joined PSA Prospective and cooperation design studio , before moving to Style Citroen.
In 2012 he joined Polyphony Digital which is known for creating the iconic « Gran Turismo » series of games for Playstation,
as a design director of its European studio.
Founded Takumi YAMAMOTO at 2017 for designing One-off Hypercars, robot, furniture and industrial products.
